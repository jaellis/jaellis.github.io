My Fiction

I've always loved fiction. While coding and statistics afford a certain kind of creativity, I hope to one day be able to devote more time to my writing.
As with all good nerds, my early writing was dystopian and sci-fi. Over the year my pieces have hewn closer to what might be called literary fiction.
